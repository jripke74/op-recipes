Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A delicouse, yet simple chocalate chip recipe, that will make you mouth water.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup semi sweet chocalate chips
  • 1 egg
  • 1 stick melted butter
  • 1 tbl real vannila
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Cream butter, eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt
  2. Add flour slowly
  3. Stir in chocalate chips
  4. Heat oven to 350 degrees
  5. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until done
