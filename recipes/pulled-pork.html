Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

A pancake recipe everyone will love. They have a taste like no other pancakes and everyone will love them.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Jiffy Mix
  • 3 cups Buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 stick melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix Jiffy mix, eggs, buttermilk and melted butter
  2. Add Jiffy or milk to desired thickness
  3. heat frying pan with butter and pour pancake mix
  4. flip when bubbles are formed throughout pancake
  5. put slab of butter on cooked side and when melted remove panckake
